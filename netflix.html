What is a cookie?
A cookie is a block of text that a Web site sends to your Internet browser (such as Internet Explorer or Netscape Navigator). To register for the Netflix service and add movies to your Rental Queue, you must activate your cookie feature in your browser. Activating Cookies in Internet Explorer 5.0

  1. Go to the "Tools" menu and select "Internet Options."
  2. Select the "Security" tab.
  3. In the "Security" section select the button that says "Custom Level.
  4. Once you select this button, you'll be in the "Security Setting" section.
  5. Scroll down to the "Cookies" section.
  6. To enable cookies, you may select "Always accept cookies" or "Prompt before accepting cookies."
  7. Click OK.
  8. "I'm Ready to Try Netflix for Free Now"
Want more control over which sites get cookies? You can control the who, why and when Web sites attempt to give you cookies by choosing "Prompt before accepting cookies." This feature will send you a message asking for your acceptance or disapproval when a Web site tries to send a cookie to your browser. When this message appears, you can simply say "yes" or "no" to accept or decline the Web site's cookie. But remember, you must accept the Netflix cookie to sign up and add items to your Rental Queue.

For any additional support related to your IE Browser you can visit: http://support.microsoft.com/


Activating Cookies in Internet Explorer 6.0

  1. Go to the "Tools" menu and select "Internet Options."
  2. Select the "Privacy" tab.
  3. The "Privacy" settings slider has six settings: Block All Cookies , High, Medium High, Medium (default level), Low, and Accept All Cookies.
  4. You may select any setting other than Block All Cookies
  5. Click OK.
  6. "I'm Ready to Try Netflix for Free Now"
For any additional support related to your IE Browser you can visit: http://support.microsoft.com/


Activating Cookies in Netscape Communicator 4
  1. Go to the "Edit" menu and select "Preferences."
  2. Click Advanced (from the vertical list on the left hand side)
  3. Click to select "Accept all cookies" or "Accept only cookies that get sent back to the originating server." If there is a check in the box next to "Warn me before storing a cookie", remove it to prevent being prompted every time a cookie needs to be written.
  4. Click OK.
  5. "I'm Ready to Try Netflix for Free Now"
